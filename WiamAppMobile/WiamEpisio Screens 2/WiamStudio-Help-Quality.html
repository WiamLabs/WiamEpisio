Why was my episode rejected for aspect ratio?

All episodes must be 9:16 vertical, 1080×1920 preferred. Horizontal footage cropped or letterboxed into a vertical frame will still fail — reframe the shot instead.

How long does review actually take?

Up to 72 hours once all completeness gates are green. If your reviewer needs longer, you'll be notified — it never goes silent past the SLA.

What counts toward the 200 soft-interest threshold?

Followers on your creator profile plus Remind Me taps on your Coming Soon teaser, combined. 50 followers alone also satisfies the gate on its own.

My trailer failed QA — what usually causes that?

Most common: wrong duration (must be 15–60s), visible watermarks from other apps, or heavy compression artifacts. Re-export at a higher bitrate and re-upload.

Can I edit an episode after it's live?

Title and description, yes — anytime. Replacing the video file on a live episode triggers a fresh quality check before it goes back live.
