Last updated: June 1, 2026 · Effective for all WiamEpisio users

1. What We Collect

We collect account details (email, phone), viewing activity, device information, and payment metadata (never full card numbers — those are handled by our payment processors).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Personalizing your Home feed and recommendations
  • Processing coin purchases and creator payouts
  • Sending notifications you've opted into
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs

3. What We Don't Do

We never sell your personal data to third parties. We don't share your watch history with advertisers outside of aggregated, anonymized trends.

4. Your Rights

You can request a copy of your data, correct inaccurate information, or delete your account entirely at any time from Settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain account data while your account is active, and for up to 90 days after deletion for fraud-prevention purposes, after which it's permanently erased.
