How pricing works

Coins are the only in-app currency

All episodes, VIP content, and unlocks are priced in coins. Coin packs are purchased with real money at the rate shown above, which may vary slightly by payment method and region.

Prices are shown in your local currency

WiamEpisio detects your region and displays coin pack prices in your local currency at checkout. The coin amount you receive never changes based on payment method.

Coins don't expire

Once purchased, coins remain in your wallet indefinitely — no forced expiry, no monthly reset.

Refund policy

Coin purchases are generally non-refundable once used to unlock content. If a payment was charged but coins weren't delivered, contact Help Center within 7 days for a review.

Exchange rates are indicative and may shift with currency markets. The price shown at checkout is always final.
