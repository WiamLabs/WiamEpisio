A free, world-class reading platform where readers discover incredible stories and creators share their voice with millions. Built in Ghana, designed for the world.

Our Story

WiamApp was born from a simple but powerful observation: Africa is home to some of the most vibrant, diverse, and compelling storytelling traditions on Earth — yet African writers have historically lacked access to the platforms, tools, and audiences that their talent deserves.

We set out to change that. WiamApp is a purpose-built novel reading platform designed from the ground up to serve African storytellers and readers, while welcoming creators and readers from every corner of the globe. Our mission is clear: make storytelling accessible, empower creators, and build the largest community of readers and writers in Africa and beyond.

Every design decision, every feature, and every line of code in WiamApp is guided by one question: Does this help writers tell better stories and readers discover them?

What Makes WiamApp Different

There are many reading apps in the world. Here's what sets WiamApp apart:

Free First, Always
Every story on WiamApp is free to start reading. The first 10 chapters of any story are always free — no exceptions. We believe in earning your attention, not gating it behind paywalls.
Built for Africa
Designed for low-bandwidth environments, mobile-first experiences, and the unique needs of African readers and creators. From mobile money payouts to offline reading support — WiamApp is made for how people actually use technology across the continent.
Creators Get Paid
Our Wiam Coins system lets readers directly support the creators they love. Qualified creators receive monthly payouts to their mobile money accounts. Your words have value — WiamApp makes sure you get it.
Safe & Moderated
WiamApp uses advanced content screening and community standards enforcement to keep the platform safe for everyone — readers, creators, and young adults alike. We take safety seriously.
Meritocracy, Not Popularity
Our WiamElite hall of fame is entirely algorithm-driven. No paid placements, no staff picks. The best stories rise based on reader engagement, ratings, and quality — nothing else.

For Readers

WiamApp is your personal reading space — a place where discovering your next favourite story takes seconds, not hours.

  • Browse by Genre: From Romance and Fantasy to African Literature, Thriller, Sci-Fi, and more — find stories that match your taste.
  • Smart Recommendations: The more you read, the better WiamApp understands your preferences. Our curation system highlights stories you'll love.
  • Reading Streaks: Build daily reading habits with streak tracking. The longer your streak, the more badges and recognition you earn.
  • Paragraph Reactions: Love a line? React to individual paragraphs with emojis. See what other readers loved too.
  • Offline Reading: Install WiamApp as a PWA and access your library even without an internet connection.
  • Beautiful Reader: Adjustable font sizes, multiple themes (dark, light, sepia), and a distraction-free reading experience designed for hours of comfortable reading.

For Creators

WiamStudio is a full-featured creative workspace that gives you everything you need to write, publish, and grow your audience.

  • Rich Text Editor: A clean, powerful editor with formatting tools, auto-save, and chapter management.
  • Instant Publishing: Write a chapter and publish it to the world with one click. No approval queues for updates — your chapters go live immediately.
  • Cover Art: Upload beautiful cover images to make your story stand out on the shelves.
  • Analytics Dashboard: Track views, unique readers, read-through rates, ratings, favourites, and engagement in real-time.
  • Monetisation: Eligible creators earn Wiam Coins from reader tips and premium chapter unlocks. Monthly payouts via mobile money.
  • Creator Bulletin: Announce updates, share thoughts, and keep your followers engaged through your personal Bulletin channel.
  • Follower Management: See who follows you, track growth, and build a loyal readership.

The Wiam Coins Economy

Wiam Coins are WiamApp's virtual currency — the bridge between readers who want to support great writing and creators who deserve to be rewarded for it.

How It Works

  • Buy Coins: Readers purchase Wiam Coins through Paystack using mobile money or card payments.
  • Spend Coins: Tip your favourite creators, unlock premium chapters (beyond Chapter 10), and show appreciation for great storytelling.
  • Creator Earnings: Creators accumulate coins from reader tips and premium unlocks. WiamApp processes monthly payouts, splitting revenue 50/50 between the creator and the platform.
  • Mobile Money Payouts: Earnings are paid directly to creators via MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, or AirtelTigo Money through Paystack's secure transfer system.

Wiam Coins never expire as long as your account is in good standing. And the first 10 chapters of every story are always free — coins are never required to start reading anything on WiamApp.

WiamElite — The Hall of Fame

WiamElite is the most prestigious recognition a story can achieve on WiamApp. It is our algorithmic hall of fame — a showcase of the very best writing the platform has to offer.

Unlike other platforms where visibility is determined by paid promotion or staff curation, WiamElite is 100% merit-based. Stories are promoted and demoted based on quantitative performance metrics:

  • Total reads and unique reader counts
  • Average rating and number of ratings
  • Reader completion rates and active engagement
  • Favourite and vote counts
  • Community standing and violation history

No payment, sponsorship, or personal connection can influence Elite status. The algorithm runs daily, and the best stories rise — pure and simple.

Safety & Community Standards

WiamApp is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and welcoming environment for all users. Our multi-layered content moderation system screens all user-submitted content against community guidelines, automatically detecting and preventing harmful material before it reaches other users.

We enforce a progressive discipline system — users who violate our guidelines receive escalating warnings, and repeated violations result in account suspension. Severe violations (threats, exploitation, doxxing) result in immediate permanent bans.

Our community reporting tools empower users to flag inappropriate content or behaviour. All reports are reviewed and acted upon promptly. We believe every reader and creator deserves a platform where they feel safe to express themselves.

Our Journey

2024
WiamApp concept begins — a Telegram bot experiment to share stories with friends. The idea of a dedicated reading platform for African storytellers takes shape.
Early 2025
WiamApp launches as a Telegram-first reading bot. Early users discover and share stories entirely within the messaging app. The community starts to grow organically.
Mid 2025
WiamApp expands to a full web application — WiamStudio launches, giving creators professional tools to write and publish. The reader experience is transformed with the web reader.
Late 2025
Major platform upgrades: Wiam Coins economy, creator monetisation, WiamElite hall of fame, Bulletin system, reading streaks, and advanced content moderation. WiamApp becomes a complete ecosystem.
2026
WiamApp 2.0 — comprehensive platform refinement, enhanced safety systems, progressive web app support, and preparation for pan-African expansion. The best is yet to come.
