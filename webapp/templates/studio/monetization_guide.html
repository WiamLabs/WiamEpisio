Monetization Guide

Everything you need to know about earning from your stories on WiamApp. Read carefully before submitting for review.

How Monetization Works

WiamApp uses a coin-based system. Readers buy coins, then spend them to unlock locked chapters. You earn a share of every coin spent on your content. Here's how it flows:

  1. 1

    Meet Eligibility Requirements

    Your account must meet all the eligibility criteria below. These ensure you're a real, active creator on the platform.

  2. 2

    Submit Your Story for Review

    Each story you want to monetize must pass an automated quality review. The review checks structure, safety, formatting, and engagement quality. You submit per-story — not per-account.

  3. 3

    Get Approved & Lock Chapters

    Once approved, you can lock chapters from Chapter 11 onward (first 10 are always free). Set a price of 3–10 coins per locked chapter.

  4. 4

    Earn Coins & Get Paid

    When readers unlock your chapters, you earn coins. Monthly payouts are sent automatically to your Mobile Money when your balance reaches the minimum threshold.

Eligibility Requirements

You must meet ALL of these requirements before you can submit any story for monetization review. Track your progress on the Creator Hub dashboard.

Account Age: 30+ days

Your account must be at least 30 days old. We need to know you're serious.

1+ Published Story

At least one story set to "Ongoing" or "Complete" status.

10+ Chapters (best story)

Your most-written story must have at least 10 published chapters.

500+ Unique Readers

Across all your stories combined. Keep writing and sharing!

100+ Followers

Build your audience. Share your work and engage with readers.

3.5+ Average Rating

With at least 20 ratings total. Quality matters.

0 Violations (60 days)

No content or community guideline violations in the past 60 days.

Trust Score: 50+

Built through consistent, positive activity on the platform.

Important: Meeting eligibility does NOT automatically monetize all your books. Each individual story must be submitted and approved separately through the Monetization Review.
Monetization Review (Per Story)

Once you're eligible, you can submit each story for an automated quality review. Our system (the Automated Tester) checks your story across 4 categories:

Structure (30 pts)

Chapter count, word count per chapter, no empty chapters, proper title & synopsis.

Safety (30 pts)

No hate speech, spam, plagiarism indicators, or prohibited content across all chapters.

Formatting (20 pts)

Proper paragraph structure, capitalization, consistent formatting, no wall-of-text.

Engagement (20 pts)

Reader interest: views, ratings, favorites. A story people actually want to read.

Minimum score: 75/100 to pass. The review checks up to 10 chapters for safety and formatting. Make sure every chapter has at least 1,000 words and is properly formatted.

What happens if you fail? You'll see your score and feedback. Fix the issues and resubmit after 24 hours. You can resubmit up to 3 times per month.

What happens if you pass? That specific story is approved for monetization. You can then lock individual chapters (Chapter 11+) from the editor's Settings panel. Repeat the process for each story you want to monetize.

Chapter Locking Rules

  • First 10 Chapters Are Always Free

    Readers can always read the first 10 chapters for free. This lets them decide if they want to continue.

  • Set Price: 3–10 Coins Per Chapter

    You choose how many coins (3 to 10) each locked chapter costs. Once a reader unlocks a chapter, it stays unlocked forever for them.

  • Premium Lock (Optional)

    You can also use Premium Lock for WiamPremium subscribers. They unlock chapters using monthly credits instead of coins.

How You Earn

Every coin spent on your locked chapters is tracked. At the end of each month, WiamApp calculates your total coins earned and converts them to real money.

Creator share: 50% of the coin value goes to you. The other 50% supports the platform. Payouts are sent via Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money) once you reach the minimum payout threshold of GHS 10.
Frequently Asked Questions
Does meeting eligibility mean all my stories are monetized?
No. Eligibility is about your account. Each story must be submitted individually for Monetization Review. Only stories that pass the review can have locked chapters.
How long does the review take?
The automated review typically completes within 24 hours. You'll see your score and result in the editor's Settings panel.
What if my story gets rejected?
You'll see your score breakdown. Fix the issues (e.g. add more words, fix formatting) and resubmit after 24 hours. You have up to 3 resubmissions per month.
Can I change chapter prices after locking?
Yes, you can unlock and re-lock chapters with different prices anytime. Readers who already unlocked keep their access.
What content is not allowed?
Hate speech, plagiarism, spam, excessive profanity, and any content violating our Community Guidelines. Please read our Terms & Conditions and Community Guidelines for full details.
How do I get more readers and followers?
Write consistently, publish regularly, engage with your community, and share your Bulletin posts. Quality content naturally attracts readers.
