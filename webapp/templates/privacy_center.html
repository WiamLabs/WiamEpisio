Privacy Center

Understand how WiamApp protects your data and your rights.

Your Privacy Matters

At WiamApp, we believe your data belongs to you. We collect only what's needed to provide you with the best reading experience, and we never sell your personal information.

What We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and any optional details you provide (bio, date of birth, pronouns, avatar). If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google.

Reading Activity

We track which stories you read, your reading progress, and your library to provide personalized recommendations. Reading streaks and bookmarks help you pick up where you left off.

Payment Data

All payments are processed by Paystack (PCI-DSS certified, backed by Stripe). We never store your card numbers or mobile money PINs. We only keep transaction records for coin balance tracking.

How We Use Your Data

To Provide Our Service

  • Personalize story recommendations based on your genres and reading history
  • Maintain your library, reading progress, and favorites
  • Process coin purchases and creator payouts
  • Send important account notifications (password resets, security alerts)

What We Never Do

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your reading history publicly (unless you choose to)
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Store payment card or mobile money credentials
Your Rights

Access & Control

  • View your data — See all your account info in Settings & Privacy
  • Edit your data — Update your profile, bio, pronouns, and preferences anytime
  • Delete your account — Permanently remove all your data from our servers
  • Deactivate — Temporarily hide your profile without losing data
  • Birthday visibility — Choose whether your birthday appears on your profile
How We Protect Your Data

Security Measures

  • All connections encrypted with HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords hashed with industry-standard bcrypt
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) available for extra security
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Payment processing by PCI-DSS certified Paystack
